外教老师一对一有哪些好处呢？

现如今很多都觉得幼儿英语外教一对一教学对孩子有着重大的意义。都想要找一个好的幼儿英语外教。那么为什么这么执着于幼儿英语外教一对一教学呢？幼儿英语外教老师一对一有哪些好处呢？

Now many of them feel that one to one teaching is of great significance to children. I want to find a good English foreign teacher. So why so persistent in children's English foreign teachers one to one teaching? What are the benefits of one to one teacher's English teacher?

一、让孩子敢于开口说英语

First, let the child dare to speak English

现在很多孩子从小就会说非常多的英语单词，他们可以和中教老师流利地说英语单词，但当面对英语外教的时候，他们害羞不敢说，害怕自己说错，这是一种学语言的心理障碍，要敢于和所有人用英语进行交流，所以幼儿英语外教课程学习很有必要。我孩子在ForeignerHR外教学习了一年了。一年之前，她的英语应试水平不错，但是很少能说出完整连贯的句子，不敢和外人交流。但是自从来了ForeignerHR外教，每天和外教一对一交流，口语水平大幅度上升了。自信说出来了，口语水平也就刷刷刷上去了。

Now many kids would say a lot of English words, and they can teach the teacher speak fluent English words, but when it comes to English teachers, they are shy to speak, afraid of their wrong, this is a kind of language mental disorder, and all people should dare to communicate in English, so English teachers it is necessary to study curriculum for young children. My child has been studying in ForeignerHR for a year. A year ago, she had a good English test, but she rarely spoke complete and coherent sentences and did not dare to communicate with outsiders. But since we have come to ForeignerHR foreign teachers, every day with foreign teachers in one - to - one communication, the level of spoken language has risen greatly. Confident say, spoken English will brush up.

二、听说能力飞速提升

Two, the ability to improve listening and speaking

语言的目的在于交流，通过天天与外教一起训练语言，进行日常生活沟通，孩子不仅能快速消除面对外教的压力，敢于开口交流，还能在外教的影响下自然而然地学会地道的英语，提高英语水平，特别是口语和听力水平。

The purpose of language is communication, through the daily training together with foreign language for communication in everyday life, children can not only quickly eliminate face teacher's pressure, dare to open communication, can naturally learn authentic English in foreign influence, improve the level of English, especially spoken English and listening comprehension.

三、从小就学会标准发音

Three, learn standard pronunciation from childhood

语言靠的是环境营造，当孩子看见中教老师，他们会选择自己擅长的中文交流，但如果是一个不懂中文的外教老师与孩子交流，孩子们会无形中运用任何表达方式与外教沟通，这就让孩子主动去学。相反，外教老师的发音，说话语音语调，词语固定搭配等都会潜移默化地影响孩子的英语学习。

The language depends on the environment, when the children saw the teacher, they will choose their own good Chinese exchanges, but if one does not understand Chinese foreign teachers to communicate with their children, the children will be any expression of communication with foreign teachers in the use of intangible, let the children take the initiative to learn. On the contrary, the pronunciation of teachers, the intonation of speech, the fixed collocation of words and phrases all have a subtle influence on the children's English learning.

四、英语教学思维西方化

Four, the Westernization of the thinking of English Teaching

英语外教一对一学习都是先经过大量的听，然后就会说了，孩子会模仿英语外教的语音与词语及句型的运用，宝贝们或许根本不知道语法原理，但是凭借语言感觉却会形成英语思维，知道怎么用英语清楚地表达，不知不觉中就成为英语小达人啦。

English teachers are the first one to one after a lot of listening learning, and then said, using voice and words and sentence patterns children imitate English teachers, kids may not know the grammar principle, but by virtue of the sense of language is the formation of English thinking, know how to express in English, imperceptibly become English Master.

总的说来，英语外教一对一教学对少儿英语学习有着很大的好处，因为相对成年人而言，孩子年龄小，可塑性强，对语言的接受能力强，一开始就有一个外教进行一对一的辅导交流，能形成下意识使用英语的习惯，从而打下一个良好的英语基础，以后学英语外教一对一也不会有太大的压力。

Generally speaking, English teachers in teaching is of great benefit to children's English learning, because of the relatively small age adults, children, plasticity, language ability, begin with a teacher one-on-one exchanges, can be formed under the consciousness of using English good English habits, so as to play the next, after learning English one teacher will not have too much pressure.
