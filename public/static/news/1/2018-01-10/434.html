幼儿英语学习，差距为何这么大

说起学英语，很多家长和孩子肯定都是一个头两个大，貌似起点相同的孩子，怎么学习起来差距就这么大呢?幼儿英语外教认为主要是以下几个关键点在发挥作用：

Speaking of learning English, many parents and children are definitely the first two children who seem to have the same starting point. How can they learn so differently?

1.对孩子的英语启蒙多早都不会太早。

1. children's English enlightenment will not be too early.

妈妈们在怀孕之后最重要的事情就是养好身体，让宝宝健康的成长，除此之外，还要注意胎教。有很多人怀疑胎教是否有用，结果是肯定的。ForeignerHR外教有多位宝妈在怀孕期间给孩子进行胎教，比如休息的时候放英语歌听，没事的时候看英文节目。对于胎儿来说，他们确实是听不懂的，但这算是一个英语环境，从胎儿时期就听英语的孩子肯定比一般的孩子对英语的适应力更强。

The most important thing for mothers to get pregnant is to keep the body well and make the baby grow healthy. Besides, we should pay attention to the prenatal education. There are many people who doubt whether the fetal education is useful or not, and the result is positive. There are many ForeignerHR teachers who teach children during pregnancy, such as listening to English songs at rest and watching English programs when they are all right. For the fetus, they really don't understand, but it's an English environment. The children who listen to English from the fetus are surely more resilient than the average children.

2.爸妈在家真的教得好孩子吗?

2. do parents really teach good children at home?

如果家长们对自己的英语水平足够自信，当然可以，但是ForeignerHR外教还是觉得专业的事情应该交给专业的人来做，家长可以给孩子一些辅助性的辅导。

If parents are confident enough for their English level, of course, but ForeignerHR foreign teachers still feel that professional matters should be given to professional people. Parents can give children some supplementary counseling.

有部分宝爸宝妈在孩子很小的时候就让孩子接触英语，比如读英语绘本，看英语动画片，唱英语儿歌，这些小动作都会成为孩子英语学习的基石。长期坚持，你会发现自己孩子的词汇量在逐步提升，语感也越来越强。

Part of Bao Bao Bao, when children were very young, exposed their children to English, such as reading picture books, watching animated cartoons and singing nursery rhymes in English, which would become the cornerstone of children's English learning. For a long time, you will find that the vocabulary of your children is gradually increasing and the sense of language is becoming stronger and stronger.

3.报培训班是真的有用还是花冤枉钱?

3. training class is really useful or wasting money?

培训班当然是有用的，毕竟人家是专业的，但前提是孩子得愿意去才行。现在家长们的教育观念都非常强，他们想让自己的孩子样样好，给孩子报一堆兴趣班的家长非常多。

Of course, the training class is useful, after all, the family is professional, but the premise is that the child is willing to go. Parents have a very strong idea of education now. They want to make their children good and have a lot of parents to give children a pile of interest classes.

就拿英语班来说，孩子在学校的时候可能就已经厌倦了背单词、做试卷，周末还要逼着他们去上课，他们能愿意吗?

Take the English class, when children are at school, they may be tired of memorizing words, making test papers, and forcing them to attend classes at weekends. Can they be willing?

ForeignerHR外教的英语班则不同，我们的外教老师都是来自英语母语国家，授课风格生动幽默，非常受孩子喜欢。我们的课堂不是传统、枯燥的教学法，而是将课堂与游戏、艺术、诗歌、绘画等结合在一起，激发孩子的学习兴趣，让孩子的学习效率得到最大提升。

The English classes of ForeignerHR foreign teachers are different. Our foreign teachers are all from native English speaking countries. The teaching style is vivid and humorous, and it is very popular with the children. Our classroom is not a traditional and boring teaching method. It combines classroom with games, art, poetry and painting to stimulate children's interest in learning and maximize their learning efficiency.

4.学英语，还是要向讲英语的人来学!

4. to learn English, or to learn English to people!

很多家长在纠结给孩子找老师的时候是中教比较好还是外教好呢?仔细想想就知道了，中教老师是采用中式思维来教孩子的，比如将汉语的发音方法、词汇记忆法、语法学习法等嫁接到英语上。而外教老师是采用英语思维来教英语，英语的连读、英语语感的培养、英文构词法等。

Many parents give children find tangled teacher is teaching good or good teachers? Think carefully to know, teacher is the use of Chinese thinking to teach children, such as pronunciation, Chinese vocabulary memory method, grammar learning method to English on grafting. While the teacher is using English thinking to teach English, English, English language training, linking English compounding.

学英语还是要跟外教学，将英语作为母语的人和将英语作为第二语言的人对于英语学习和认知是完全不同的，要想孩子学到纯正的英语口语和发音，外教是您的不二选择。

To learn English or to teach outside, people who speak English as a native language and people who use English as a second language are quite different from English learning and cognition. If you want children to learn pure spoken English and pronunciation, foreign teachers are your choice.
