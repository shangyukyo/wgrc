学霸是如何学好英语，实现留学目标的？

去国外留学，开阔眼界，更新思维是很多国内的学霸早早就在心里默默定下的目标。在接下来的日子里，就是为了这个目标日复一日的努力。这些学霸们都是如何一步步实现自己的目标的呢？来看看外教今天的分享：来自英国贝尔法斯特女王大学读硕士的女学霸的求学经验吧。

Go abroad, broaden their horizons, update the thinking is a lot of domestic Curve Wrecker early on in my heart silently goals. In the next day, it is for the goal of the day after day. How do these bullies realize their goals step by step? Take a look at the share of the foreign teachers today: the study experience of the master of women from The Queen's University of Belfast in England.

设定目标，并为此努力

Set goals and work hard for it

适时为自己“确立一个小目标”，并想方设法去达成它，就迈出了成功的第一步。小莹为了自己的留学梦，坚持了4年之久。在读高中时就有出国留学的打算，所以本科选择读中英合办的3+1双学位课程。为了打好语言基础，利用在校的3年时间苦学英语，并且提前学习了部分专业课程。

The first step in success is to "establish a small goal" for yourself and try to achieve it. Xiao Ying has persisted for 4 years for his dream of studying abroad. In high school, there is a plan to study abroad, so I choose to read both Chinese and English 3+1 double degree courses. In order to lay a good foundation of language, 3 years of learning English in school, and advance learning some professional courses.

为了心中的“大目标”，先设定一个一个的小目标，并一个一个的攻破。最终成就了那个“大目标”。

For the "big goal" in the heart, first set a small target, and one to break. The ultimate achievement of the "big goal". .

多听、多说、多练

More listening, more speaking, and more practice

小莹说，一开始到学校，因为当地的人说话有口音，一开始也听不懂。坚持多听，慢慢也就懂了。她告诉我们，学习语言至关重要的因素是“听”。看英剧、听BBC、和当地人聊天等都是她日常生活的一部分。

Xiao Ying said that the beginning of the school, because the local people speak accent, at the beginning, can not understand. Keep listening, and understand it slowly. She told us that the most important factor in learning the language is "listening." Watching English plays, listening to BBC, and chatting with the local people are all part of her daily life.

在课堂上，老师也鼓励学生多发言。课上有很多小组讨论，意在让学生自由表达自己的想法，陈述自己的观点。在老师的鼓励下，渐渐适应了这样的上课方式。

In the classroom, the teacher also encourages the students to speak more. There are many groups of discussions in the class intended to allow students to express their ideas freely and to state their views. Under the encouragement of the teacher, it gradually adapted to the way of class.

多阅读

Multi reading

除了听和说，阅读也是提高语言能力的关键因素。读书不仅可以提高阅读能力，还可以提高表达和写作能力。书读得多了，整个思维都会有变化。

In addition to listening and speaking, reading is also a key factor in improving language ability. Reading can not only improve the reading ability, but also improve the ability to express and write. The book is much read, and the whole mind will change.

关于阅读，外教老师有几点建议：首先要选择自己感兴趣并与个人阅读水平相当的书。在阅读中遇到生词，不建议一遇到生词就查，因为这样会影响阅读速度和乐趣。可以先自己猜测词义，事后再查字典。

With regard to reading, the teacher of foreign teachers has several suggestions: first of all, we should choose a book that is interested in and is equivalent to the level of personal reading. When you encounter a new word in reading, you do not recommend a new word, because it will affect the speed and pleasure of reading. You can first guess the meaning of the word, then look up the dictionary afterwards.

希望外教老师的分享对大家的学习能有所帮助，每个人都收到理想中的学校offer。

It is hoped that the sharing of teachers from foreign teachers will be helpful to everyone's study, and everyone has received the ideal school offer.
