学英语的目的达到了吗？

这个冬天你学英语了吗?跟外教学英语就像是过圣诞节一样流行，幼儿英语外教之所以越来越多，越来越受欢迎，对于小朋友来说，他们年龄太小还不懂得学英语的目的是什么，但是家长需要在英语学习上给孩子引导，明确以下几个学习目的尤其重要。

This winter you learn English? To teaching English is like a Christmas like pop, children's English teacher is more and more, more and more popular for children, what is their age is too young to understand the purpose of learning English, but parents need to guide children in learning English, clear the following the purpose of learning is especially important.

一、培养兴趣

First, cultivate interest

任何知识的学习都是以兴趣为前提，没有兴趣的学习是累赘、是负担，尤其不适合小孩子。孩子学英语完全不知道目的在哪，主要的原因就是为了兴趣。英语小游戏、英语绘本阅读、英文儿歌等都会激发他们学习的兴趣。我们的幼儿英语外教一般会根据孩子的学习兴趣点进行有针对性的教学。

The learning of any knowledge is based on interest, and learning without interest is cumbersome and burdened, especially for children. Children learn English completely without knowing where the purpose is, the main reason is for interest. English games, English picture book reading, English children's songs and so on will stimulate their interest in learning. Our children's English foreign teachers generally carry out targeted teaching according to their children's learning interest points.

二、养成学习习惯

Two, develop learning habits

学习习惯的养成不仅是对于英语学习，对于个人性格的塑造都有重要的作用。比如学习时要全神贯注，不能三心二意;每次上外教课之前要有预习的习惯，课后要有复习的习惯;上课结束后要整理自己的文具和书本等等。看似都是很小的事情，但要养成这些良好的习惯也不是一件容易的事情。

The formation of learning habits is not only for English learning, but also plays an important role in the shaping of personal character. For example, when learning to be absorbed in, can not be of two minds before each lesson teach; must have the habit of preview, after-school review habits; end of the class should arrange their own stationery and books and so on. It seems to be very small, but it is not easy to develop these good habits.

幼儿英语外教一开始会陪着孩子一起去做这些事情，比如一起预习、复习，一起整理书桌等，等到孩子养成习惯为止。另外，在整个学习的过程中，还要教孩子哪些做法是正确的，哪些是错误的等。

Preschool English teachers begin to do these things with their children at the beginning, such as preparing together, reviewing, tidying up desks, etc. until children develop habits. In addition, in the whole process of learning, we should also teach the children what is correct and what is wrong.

三、形成语感

Three, form a sense of language

语感对于英语学习尤其重要，有很多人在阅读的时候可能并不知道每一个单词的意义，但只要语感够好，依然可以做对课后题目，这就来源于语感。幼儿阶段的孩子有很强的语言塑造性，可以通过多说、多练的形式来培养语感。

Language sense is very important for English learning. Many people may not know the meaning of every word when they read, but as long as they feel good enough, they can still do after-school questions, which stems from language sense. Children in early childhood have a strong language shape. They can cultivate a sense of language through more and more practice.

四、听力启蒙

Four, hearing enlightenment

有很多成人不敢说、不会说英语跟小时候没有做好听力启蒙有关系，说话人的语速稍快一些就会听不懂。小孩子的模仿能力很强，听觉敏锐，外教老师在授课的过程中一般会让孩子模仿单词或语句的发音，训练扎实的听力基础。

A lot of adults dare not say, can't speak English and do not do good hearing enlightenment when they are young, the speaker's voice is slightly faster and will not understand. Children's imitation ability is very strong, and their hearing is acute. In the process of teaching, teachers usually imitate children's pronunciation of words or sentences, and train a solid listening foundation.

这也是培养孩子自信心的一个方面，当孩子能听懂外教老师的语言后，他们就会很愿意跟外教交流，对英语学习产生自信。

This is also an aspect of training children's self-confidence. When children can understand the language of foreign teachers, they will be very willing to communicate with foreign teachers and have confidence in English learning.

五、积累词汇

Five. Accumulating vocabulary

词汇是英语学习的基础，只有积累足够的词汇量才能达到英语水平的提升。对于幼儿来说，他们的记忆力好，时间丰富，家长可以充分利用这些时间来帮助孩子提高词汇量。但是积累词汇的方法可并不是背单词哦，这会让孩子反感英语学习。

Vocabulary is the basis of English learning, and only a sufficient amount of vocabulary can be accumulated to improve the English level. For children, their memory is good and time is rich, and parents can make full use of these time to help their children improve their vocabulary. But the way to accumulate vocabulary is not to memorize words, which makes the child dislike English learning.

少儿英语外教一般是在学习的过程中采取逐渐渗透的形式教孩子增加词汇，并且教学形式不局限于书本。像课外活动、英语游戏、英语会话等都是非常有效的方法。

Children's English foreign teachers generally teach children to increase their vocabulary in the process of learning, and the form of teaching is not limited to books. Such as extracurricular activities, English games, English conversations, etc. are all very effective methods.
