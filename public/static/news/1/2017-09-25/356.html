外教一对一量身定制孩子的英语课程_外教_ForeignerHR

随着中华五千年的历史流传，每一位家长都想给孩子最好的教育，都说再穷不能穷教育，家长们想给孩子的甚至是如“贵族”一般的教育。跟传统意义上的大班课做比较，英语外教一对一或许就是如“贵族”般的教育。

As China's 5000 year old history spreads, every parent wants to give his children the best education, and he says that he can not afford to be poor and educated, and that parents want to give their children even the education of the nobility. Compared with the large class in the traditional sense, English teachers may be educated like "aristocrat".

跟我们传统的理解有所不同，贵族教育、贵族精神并不是和平民教育、平民精神对立的，这里指的当然也不是金钱方面。真正的贵族，应该是拥有自制力和强烈社会责任感的，以及强大精神力量的。

Contrary to our traditional understanding, aristocratic education and aristocratic spirit are not opposed to civilian education or the spirit of the common people, nor is it, of course, money. True nobility should have self-control and a strong sense of social responsibility, as well as strong mental strength.

这句话是英国最负盛名的公学办学精神之一。在英国，中产阶级家长一般都会把孩子送到“公学”，即英国为贵族和资产阶级子女特别开设的独立中等学校。这些学校培养学生的目的是将她们送入世界上有名的大学，培养对社会有贡献的人才，这也从某种意义上代表了真正的“贵族学校”。

This is one of Britain's most prestigious public schools. In the UK, middle-class parents generally send their children to "public schools", an independent secondary school for the nobility and bourgeois children. The purpose of these schools to train students is to send them into famous universities in the world and to cultivate talents who contribute to society. This also represents a real "noble school" in a sense".

真正的贵族精神，应该有三根重要的支柱：

The real spirit of nobility should have three important pillars:

一是文化的教养，拥有丰富的知识内涵，能够抵御物质主义的诱惑，培育高贵的道德情操与文化精神。

First, the cultivation of culture, has a wealth of knowledge, can resist the temptation of materialism, and cultivate noble moral sentiments and cultural spirit.

二是社会的担当和责任感。富于自律性，不仅在知识和技能上是社会精英，还要勇于担当，帮助弱势群体，担当起国家的责任和使命。

Two, social responsibility and sense of responsibility. Full of self-discipline, not only in knowledge and skills is the social elite, but also the courage to play, to help vulnerable groups, to assume the responsibility and mission of the state.

三是自由的灵魂，意志独立，不卑不亢。是自由与道德的化身，不追逐潮流，不为政治强权与多数人的意见所奴役。

The three is a free spirit, independent will be neither humble nor pushy. It is the embodiment of freedom and morality, not chasing the tide, and not enslaving the opinions of the political power and the majority.

四是英语，英语是世界语言，是通向世界文化的钥匙。从小培养孩子的英语能力，让孩子勇敢说，大胆说，为以后的发展奠定基础。

Four is English, English is the language of the world, and the key to the world culture. Cultivate children's English ability from childhood, let children speak bravely, speak boldly and lay the foundation for future development.

英语外教一对一是当下最适合孩子学习英语的方式，我们摒弃了传统大课的劣势，结合孩子的自身基础和学习目的，量身定制属于孩子的英语课程。

English teachers, one to one, is the most suitable way for children to learn English, we abandon the disadvantages of traditional major courses, combined with the child's own foundation and learning purposes, tailored to children's English courses.
