练习商务英语听力的步骤

对于想学商务英语的人来说，听力显得尤其重要，如果听不懂，那书面语再好也都不行啊。很多同学表示自从走出校园，商务英语急需提高，以下是我们的商务英语外教介绍的几个提高大法，希望可以帮到有需要的同学们。

For people who want to learn business English, listening is especially important. If you don't understand it, the written language is not good. Many students say that business English is in urgent need of improvement since going out of campus. Here are some improvement methods introduced by our business English teacher, hoping to help students in need.

练习商务英语听力时应按照以下几个步骤来：

When practicing business English listening, we should follow the following steps:

Step 1：

Step 1:

第一遍一定要认真、仔细的听，但并不是说每个单词都要听懂，而是要在第一遍的时候搞清楚故事发生的背景和人物关系。另外要全神贯注，就像是自己在考试一样，不要想着第一遍没听懂还有第二遍，这种依赖症对练习听力很不利。

First time, we must listen carefully and carefully, but it doesn't mean that every word needs to be understood, but we need to find out the background and the relationship between people in the first time. In addition, be absorbed in the whole, like yourself in the exam, do not think about the first time not to understand the second times, this dependence on listening to practice is very bad.

Step 2：

Step 2:

脱离文字材料再接着听，如果遇到听不懂的单词，可以按住重复键多听几遍，尽量不要去看原文。如果听几遍之后还是弄不懂，可以试着拼写出来，听力结束后再去查。如果觉得自己已经足够熟悉文字材料，可以在听力的过程中迅速跟着默写出重要的单词，让自己可以串联起整个故事。

Get out of the text and listen. If you meet a word that you don't understand, you can hold down the repeat keys a few more times and try not to look at the original. If you can't understand it after a few times, try to spell it out and check it after the hearing. If you think you have enough familiar text materials, can quickly follow the written out important words in the listening process, so that they can form the story.

Step 3：

Step 3:

跟着阅读材料一起大声朗读。商务英语外教说，不要觉得练习听力就跟朗读没有关系，其实自己朗读自己听也是锻炼听力的过程啊。

Read aloud with the reading material. Business English foreign teachers say, do not feel that the practice of hearing is not related to reading aloud, in fact, reading their own listening to listening is also the process of listening.
