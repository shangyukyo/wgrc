为什么你的英语学不好？

请问你英语学习有多久了?如果有人问你这个问题，感觉英语学习也有差不多十来年的时间了。但是你学习的英语究竟有没有用到呢?私人英语外教越来越普遍，很大一部分的原因是需要针对性提高的人群越来越多。今天我们就来看看为什么你的英语总是学不好。

How long have you been learning English? If someone asks you this question, you feel like you have English learning for about ten years. But whether you learn English or not? Private English teachers are becoming more and more popular. A large part of the reason is that more and more people need to be targeted. Today, let's see why you don't always learn English well.

1、操之过急

1, act too hastily

很多成人年学英语都是有明确的目的的，但是操之过急只会导致事倍功半。求学心切的心理是可以理解的，但语言学习是一个相对漫长的积累过程。私人外教老师建议我们重视点滴积累，每天抽出一定的时间来学习，并做一个比较详尽的计划，如怎样练习口语，怎样提升语法，稳中求进。

Learn English for many years adults have a clear purpose, but will only lead to not act with undue haste. Learning psychology is understandable, but language learning is a relatively long process of accumulation. The teacher suggested that we attach importance to private foreign teachers to accumulate, take some time to learn every day, and do a more detailed plan, such as how to practise oral English. How to improve the grammar, while maintaining stability.

2、基础差

2, base difference

基础差并不是阻碍我们学习成功的致命因素，但如果基础差却又激进学习就不好了。有些同学可能觉得自己已经有很多年的英语学习基础了，甚至四六级已经过了，还能说基础差吗?当然，如果你已经过了四六级，但仍然达不到跟外国人正常交流日常英语的地步，这样能算是基础好吗。基础差并不是多大的难事，找到学习方法，一步步的巩固才是重要的。如果在学习的过程中能得到专业外教老师的辅导就更好了，学习效果也会更加显著。

Base difference is not a fatal factor to hinder our success in learning, but it is not good to study radically if the foundation is poor. Some students may feel that you have basic English for many years, or even 46 have passed, but also said that poor? Of course, if you are over 46, but still not up to the normal daily communication with foreigners in English. This is the foundation please. Foundation difference is not a big difficult task, it is important to find a way to learn and consolidate step by step. If you can learn in the process of professional foreign teacher counseling is better, the learning effect will be more significant.

3、怕说，不敢说

3, afraid to say, dare not say

这是很多人学英语的一个通病，学的是哑巴英语。特别是对于无法接触英语语言环境的人来说，这个问题就更需要解决。忙工作、忙学习，忙来忙去有可能什么都没收获。我们学习英语就是要达到使用的目的。参加一些英语角的活动，找英语外教老师学习英语，与友人一起用英语交谈等都是不错的学习方法。在没有语言环境的时候要想着去创造，这样才能提高自己的英语能力。

This is a common problem for many people to learn English, learning is dumb english. Especially for people who can't touch the English language environment, this problem needs to be solved. Busy work, busy study, busy busy, may not harvest anything. We are learning English to achieve the purpose of use. Taking part in some English corner activities, looking for English foreign teachers to learn English, talking with friends in English, etc., are good learning methods. In the absence of language environment, want to create, so as to improve their English ability.
