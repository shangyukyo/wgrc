这些方法可以快速提高孩子的英语口语

英语口语外教越来越多正表现出广大家长对于孩子英语口语的重视。作为世界通用语言之一，英语将世界上所有的人都联系在一起。可是不得不承认，很多中国的孩子英语读写能力较强，听说能力较差，今天就让我们和英语外教一起来看看怎样才能有效的提高孩子的口语水平。

Spoken English teacher more and more, is showing the majority of parents for children's spoken English attention. As one of the most common languages in the world, English connects all the people in the world. However, I have to admit that many Chinese children have strong ability to read and write English and have poor listening and speaking ability. Today let us join the English teachers to see how we can effectively improve their spoken english.

1、教你的孩子使用字典

1 teach your child to use a dictionary

使用字典怎么能提高英语口语呢?我们都知道，孩子在小学阶段的时候就会学习使用汉语字典，这可以丰富他们对于汉语的认识，学习英语亦如此。如果孩子已经有一定的英语基础，可以尝试着让孩子看英英字典，字典里会用简单的英文单词来做解释，有利于孩子形成英语思维。

How can we improve spoken English with a dictionary? We all know that when children are in primary school, they will learn to use Chinese dictionaries, which can enrich their understanding of Chinese and learn English as well. If the children have some foundation in English, can try to let the children see the English dictionary, the dictionary will use simple words to explain English, is conducive to the formation of children thinking in english.

2、在家里和孩子用英语交流

2. Communicate with children in English at home

语言环境对于孩子学英语非常重要，目前的研究来看，有不少家长都是会英语的，也许还不能达到商务英语的层次，但是日常生活中的简单对话是没有问题的。孩子在学校里已经学习了英语，回到家就是一个很好的语言环境，比如吃饭的时候、选购商品的时候都可以用简单的英文跟孩子对话，锻炼他们开口说的能力。

The language environment is very important for children to learn English, the present study, there are many parents speak English, may not be able to reach the level of business English, but a simple conversation in daily life is not a problem. The children have been learning English in school, home is a good language environment, such as dinner, buy goods can be used in simple English dialogue with the children, they exercise the ability to speak.

3、口音纯正的外教

3, pure accent foreign teacher

选择合适的外教老师必不可少，在家里说的英语，在学校里学到的语法，可能都带有汉化的色彩，孩子的模仿能力强，口音尚未形成，外教老师可以在这个关键时期起到举足轻重的作用。北京唐氏国际文化外教精选了英美国家、经验丰富的外教老师，可以为孩子量身打造属于他的学习方案，让你的孩子“语出惊人”，练就一口纯正的英语口语。

Select the appropriate teacher essential English spoken in the home, learn in school grammar, may have with Chinese color, children's ability to imitate, accent has not yet formed, teachers can play an important role in this critical period. Beijing Tang International Cultural teachers selected teachers and experienced in Anglo American countries, can be tailored for the children of his own study plan, let your child "stunned", have a pure english.

4、大声朗读可以帮助孩子提高口语能力

4, reading aloud can help children improve their oral English

家长可以帮助孩子挑选一些美文或者课本里的一些摘文，诗歌或者故事也是不错的选择，每天早晨让孩子大声的朗读出来。长期的朗读外文资料无疑是可以帮助孩子们丰富他们的词汇量以及提高他们的英语口语技能的。

Parents can help children pick out some beautiful essays or some extracts in their textbooks. Poetry or stories are also a good choice. Let them read aloud every morning. Reading a foreign language for a long time is no doubt helpful for children to enrich their vocabulary and improve their oral English skills.

5、内容科学的课程

5, content science courses

想要提高孩子的英语口语能力，只靠着念课本肯定是不行的，科学的课程设置可以提高学习效率，激发孩子的学习兴趣。北京唐氏国际文化的幼少儿英语外教老师本着“好玩有趣、动手开口”的准则，让课堂变得生动有趣。我们打造让孩子兴致盎然的学习情境，在实景训练中提高孩子的英语口语能力。

If you want to improve your oral English ability, you can't do it only by reading textbooks. The scientific curriculum can improve your learning efficiency and stimulate your child's interest in learning. Beijing Down's international culture of young children oral English teacher, in accordance with the "fun, fun, hands-on" guidelines, so that the classroom becomes vivid and interesting. We create children's interest in the learning situation, in the actual training to improve children's oral English ability.
