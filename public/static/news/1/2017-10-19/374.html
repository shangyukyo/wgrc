有没有有效英语的背诵方法呢?

学习一门新的语言，背诵肯定是必不可少的。但是你真的会背诵吗?有没有有效的背诵方法呢?我们的一对一英语外教老师表示有效的背诵不仅可以强化基础知识，还可以培养语感。

Learning a new language, recitation must be essential. But do you really recite? Is there an effective way of reciting? Our one to one English teacher says that effective recitation can not only strengthen the basic knowledge, but also cultivate the language sense.

a、影子背诵法

A and shadow recitation

这是比较有效的方法之一，当然也是比较费脑子的了，但是脑子如果不动起来，怎么背诵呢?私人外教告诉我们，可以先自己背诵好一段文章，在自己不是很熟练的时候就打开录音机，跟着录音机后面背诵，大概可以保持2~3个单词的距离。这样的背诵可以帮你调整发音，还可以提升听力水平。

This is one of the effective methods, of course is relatively tricky, but if you don't mind moving, how to memorize? Private foreign teachers told us to recite a passage, then turn on the recorder in his not very skilled, with the tape recorder back to recite, can probably keep 2~3 word distance. This kind of recitation can help you tune your pronunciation and improve your listening ability.

b、想象背诵法

B and imaginative recitation

有些人肯定会觉得背诵是一件特别无聊的事情，私人外教建议这些小伙伴可以想象自己身处在文中的语境里，甚至可以到真实的语境中。比如今天背诵的是关于饭店用餐的文章，完全可以在餐厅里背诵，还可以用刚刚背诵到的句子和自己的小伙伴对话。

Some people will feel that reciting is a very boring thing. Private teachers suggest that these little partners can imagine themselves in the context of the text, even in the real context. For example, today's recitation is about hotel dining, which can be recited in a restaurant, and can also be recited with a dialogue with a small partner.

c、零碎时间背诵法

C and fragmentary time recitation

之前经常听说有某些学霸经常把最新学到的单词或者语法记在小卡片上，一有时间就拿出来看，有些人会觉得这种做法会不会太傻，但私人外教表示零碎时间背诵法确实有效。将你要背诵的篇目拆分成好几个小段，每次可以记诵一部分，把他们抄写在小纸条上，等车、睡觉前后等碎片时间都可以拿出来背一背。

Often heard before there are some Curve Wrecker often put the newly learned words or grammar written on a small card, a time to come see, some people will feel that this approach will not be too silly, but that time private foreign teachers reciting method is effective. Will you have to recite the contents split into several pieces, each part can be memorized, copying them in a small piece of paper, such as cars, sleep before and after fragments of time can take out the back.

d、一口气背诵法

D, one breath recitation

背诵英语时总是停顿，还一直嗯嗯...啊啊?想必很多人在背诵的时候都会出现这样的情形，一方面是自己不够熟练，另一方面是因为自己的语气词太多，这样的背诵总是不尽如人意，不能给人流利的感觉。私人英语外教建议我们尝试一口气背诵法，一口气读完一个句子，连续多读几遍，句子中间不要停顿，直到自己可以不卡克的背诵完一整句话。

Always pause to recite English, has also been... Ah ah? Must have a lot of people in the recitation will appear when this situation, one is not skilled, on the other hand is because of too much of their words, this recitation is not satisfactory, can give people good feeling. Private English teacher suggested that we try a recitation, read a sentence, continuous read several times, the middle of a sentence without pause, recite all the sentence until they can not Kark.

e、同声传译背诵法

E and simultaneous interpreting recitation

这是小编在学生时代最喜欢用的背诵方法之一，和自己的同桌商量好，大家在固定的时间内一起背诵，然后请小伙伴说中文，你立刻翻译成英文;再请他说英文，你立刻翻译成中文;最后再互换角色。这个方法的好处是帮助你在想不起来下文的时候，会先想到中文，然后迅速的翻译成英文并背诵出来。

This is a small part of the students in love with one of the times the way of reciting his deskmate, and discuss the good, we recite together in a fixed period of time, then please say Chinese buddy, you immediately translated into English; then he said please English, you immediately translated into Chinese; then swap roles. The advantage of this method is to help you when you can't think of the following, will first think of Chinese, and then quickly translated into English and recite it.

f、听写背诵法

F, dictation and recitation

有很多同学明明已经背诵完了一篇文章，但是如果在录音机里听到还是感觉不能完全理解每一个单词呢?这是因为你的发音不够准确。可以尝试着一边听录音，一遍将你听到的句子默写下来，这样记诵的时候就不用太着急速度，而是要听懂每一个单词并正确的默写。

There are many students who have finished reciting an article, but if you hear or feel not fully understand each word in the tape recorder, this is because your pronunciation is not accurate enough. Try listening to recordings, again will you hear sentence dictation down, so do not worry too much memory speed, but to understand every word and correct dictation.
