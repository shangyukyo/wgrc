如何选择英语培训机构

　　现在在一线城市工作，需要用到英语的地方特别多。英语能力强，在日常生活中更容易交到外国朋友，在公司也更有利于职场的发展。除了公司员工自发的选择英语学习外。企业为了更好的与国际接轨，很多公司也会选择给员工安排商务英语培训。商务英语培训，是企业和员工共同进步的双赢。正因为如此，越来越多的企业开始找外教老师开展商务英语培训，那么在选择商务英语培训时要注意些什么呢？


　　Now in the first tier cities, there are more places to use English. English ability is strong, it is easier to make foreign friends in daily life. In the company, the company is also more conducive to the development of the workplace. In addition to the spontaneous selection of English learning by the employees of the company. In order to better meet the international standards, many companies will also choose to arrange business English training for their employees. Business English training is a win-win situation for enterprises and employees to make progress together. Because of this, more and more enterprises begin to look for foreign teachers to carry out business English training. So what should we pay attention to in choosing business English training?


　　一、英语培训机构的资质


　　I. qualification of English training institutions


　　选择一家商务英语培训机构，通常要考虑其资质。资质代表了培训机构的可信度和专业度。是否具有大型企业的培训经验，是否有真实案例可以参考，学员对机构的评价怎么样？重点看课程是怎么样安排的？是不是量身定制课程，有没有具体的培训方案。这些都是需要了解的。


　　Choosing a business English training institution usually takes into account its qualifications. Qualifications represent the credibility and professionalism of the training institutions. Do you have the training experience of large enterprises, whether there is a real case for reference, and how do students evaluate the organization? How do we focus on the arrangement of the course? Is it a tailored course, and there is no specific training program. These are all needs to be understood.


　　二、英语培训机构的师资


　　Two. Teachers of English training institutions


　　师资力量直接决定了培训效果。企业英语培训与平常的英语培训不一样。培训的老师除了要有过硬的英语水平外，也需要了解培训时所涉及的相关行业知识。所以，是否拥有商务英语培训经验这一点也很重要。


　　The effectiveness of the training is directly determined by the teachers' strength. Business English training is not the same as ordinary English training. In addition to having a strong English level, the training teachers also need to understand the related industry knowledge involved in the training. Therefore, it is also important to have the experience of business English training.


　　三、培训方案是否系统


　　Three. Whether the training program is systematic or not


　　培训方案整个过程有没有完整的计划，例如：培训需求分析、开课前的测评、反馈、调研、调整等，有没有一定的预期目标。系统完整的培训方案是上好培训课的第一步。


　　Is there a complete plan for the whole training process, such as training needs analysis, pre assessment, feedback, research and adjustment? A complete system of training program is the first step in a good training course.


　　四、英语培训的授课方式


　　Four. The teaching methods of English training


　　现在主流的授课模式主要有在线课程和面授课程。简单来说，在线课程比较便宜，但学习效果不如面授。不过对上班族来说，面授课程推荐选择外教上门授课。因为大家白天上了一天班，已经很累了。外教上门授课可以节省时间和精力，以便更好的投入到学习中。


　　Now the mainstream teaching mode mainly online courses and face-to-face courses. In simple terms, online course is cheaper, but the effect is not as good as face-to-face learning. But for office workers, face-to-face courses recommended foreign teachers teach. Because everyone has a day in the sky, it is already very tired. Teaching at home can save time and energy so as to better invest in the study.

　　综合来说，外教老师上门授课的方式最适合企业英语培训了。在汉西外教，有很多知名企业在给员工做商务英语培训时，都会优先选择外教上门授课的方式。因为汉西外教有很多具有丰富的商务英语培训经验的老师。新老学员的口碑都是很不错的。如果你想提升自己的商务英语水平，可以来汉西外教免费试听外教的英语课哦。想要学好英语，想要更好的职场发展，就赶快行动吧！
