英语培训哪家比较好？

在教学过程中，通过什么手段能够达到培养孩子能力的目的？其中比较敏感的问题就是教师。一个好的培训机构老师自然是关键，面对培训机构打出的师资力量广告，不少家长面临如何选择的困惑。据了解，目前少儿市场中存在着三种师资使用形式：纯外教、纯中教和中外混授。中国教师优点在于讲语法水平很高，外教优势则在于发音、口语表达，对孩子学习英语口语很有帮助。

In the course of teaching, what means can be achieved to achieve the purpose of cultivating the ability of children? The more sensitive issue is the teacher. A good training institution teacher is the key, and many parents face the confusion of how to choose. It is understood that at present, there are three forms of teacher use in the children's market: pure foreign education, pure Chinese teaching and mixed teaching at home and abroad. The advantage of Chinese teachers lies in the high level of grammar. The advantages of foreign teachers are pronunciation and oral expression. It is very helpful for children to learn oral English.

现在家长们很烦恼很纠结的一个问题是：如何为孩子选择一家靠谱又合心意的少儿英语机构。外教在英语培训机构摸爬滚打8余载，有点发言权，以下提出8点供各位家长们参考：

One of the questions that parents are worried about now is how to choose a reliable and supportive children's English institution for their children. The teacher fought in the English training institutions for more than 8 years, a little voice, following puts forward 8 points for your parents:

1、注意教材（或原版教材）的权威性、系统性、科学性

1. Pay attention to the authority, systematicness and scientificity of the textbook (or the original edition)

家长选择培训机构，要好好考察教材的质量，重要衡量原则有三――权威性、系统性、科学性。毕竟，教育这种无形效劳不同于有形产品，每一个环节、元素都对教育质量起着决议性的作用，教材是教育机构教学的根据和根底，教材的权威性、系统性、科学性，直接影响孩子的学习兴趣和效果。建议家长在选择教育机构时，严格审查教育机构所用教材。

Parents choose training institutions to examine the quality of teaching materials well. The important measurement principles are three - authoritative, systematic and scientific. After all, the education service is different from tangible products and intangible elements, each link, the quality of education plays a decisive role, teaching material is the basis and mechanism of education teaching, authoritative, systematic and scientific teaching, directly affect children's learning interest and effect. It is suggested that parents should strictly examine the teaching materials used by educational institutions when choosing educational institutions.

2、严格调查机构背景，避免被误导高质量的少儿英语机构，势必会有大量的、专业的投入，包括员工、师资、课程研发、教材、教学地点、资金等等。因此，身靠强大的集团集团背景，是教学质量的有力保证。关于少儿机构的集团背景，家长可进行详尽调查包括教育资质、机构成立时间、运营时间（至少十年以上）、员工人数、师资配比、教学成果、教育理念等等。

2, we must strictly investigate the background of institutions and avoid being misled to high quality children's English institutions. There are bound to be a large number of professional inputs, including staff, teachers, curriculum development, teaching materials, teaching sites, funds and so on. Therefore, the background of a strong group group is a powerful guarantee for the quality of teaching. With regard to the group background of children's institutions, parents can conduct detailed surveys, including educational qualification, establishment time and operation time (at least ten years or more), number of employees, ratio of teachers, teaching outcomes, educational ideas and so on.

3、教育理念是否先进直接影响孩子学习效果

3. Whether the educational idea is advanced directly affects the children's learning effect

关于少儿英语的教育理念问题上，有各种各样的说法，综观学习英语的这些理念中，笔者比较认可的是学科英语的教学法，这一方法源于加拿大浸入式教学，后引入中国，不断丰富完善，成为少儿英语学习法中较为奏效的方法论。

The problem about children's English education idea, there are all kinds of stories, in the English learning of these ideas, the author compares the approval is the subject of teaching method of English, this method originated in Canada immersion teaching, after the introduction of Chinese, constantly enrich and perfect, become the method of children's English learning method is effective on.

4、看是否离家比较近

4. See if you are close to home

学习英语是一个长期坚持、不断累积的过程，孩子学习英语也是如此。因此，家长在给孩子找英语培训班的时候一定要找离家比较近的培训学校。家长一般给孩子找英语培训学校都是平时挤出来的时间。如果选择离自己家比较远的地方，那么接送孩子上下课是很花时间的，如果是短期的培训还好，坚持一段时间就过去了，可是孩子学习英语是一个长期的过程。

Learning English is a long-term persistence and continuous process of learning English as well. Therefore, parents must find training schools that are close to their home when they are looking for English training classes for their children. It is time for parents to find English training schools for their children. If you choose a place far away from your home, it will take a lot of time to pick up and take up classes. If it's a short-term training, it will be fine for a long time, but it will be a long process for children to learn English.

5、看师资力量

5. Look at the teacher's strength

一般培训机构都有中教和外教。在选择培训学校的时候，一定要选择一个中外教结合的培训机构。因为孩子在没有任何基础的情况下必须要选择中教，毕竟中教老师才能够与孩子进行中英交流的，而外教不能。外教只能跟孩子进行纯英语交流，如果没有英语基础的孩子在纯外教的班级上课，那就跟看哑巴电影没有任何区别。

The general training institutions have both Chinese and foreign teachers. When choosing a training school, we must choose a training organization that combines Chinese and foreign education. Because children have to choose teaching in the absence of any basis, after all, teachers can communicate with the children in Chinese and English, but foreign teachers can not. Foreign teachers can only communicate with children in pure English. If there is no English based children in pure foreign classes, there is no difference from watching dumb movies.

6、调查教学环境家长将孩子送到专业的少儿英语教育机构进行英语启蒙，平安问题是重中之重。少儿英语学校大都有良好的装修，以便为孩子提供一个轻松的、充满兴趣和欢声笑语的学习环境。但是装修也会带来一些问题，如异味，孩子生动好动，一旦追逐打闹，能否有采取防护措施，避免孩子跌倒、受伤，倡议家长多停止实地调查，再做决议。

6, the parents of the teaching environment will send their children to the professional children's English education institutions for English Enlightenment, and the safety problem is the most important. Children's English schools mostly have good decoration to provide children with a relaxed, interesting and happy learning environment. But decoration will also bring some problems, such as peculiar smell, lively and lively children. Once we chase and fight, whether we can take protective measures to prevent children from falling or getting hurt, we suggest that parents stop on-site investigation and make resolutions.

7、进行免费试听少儿英语机构一般都会布置免费的Demo试听课，家长普通可拨打免费咨询电话，进行预约，选择周末带孩子去实地试听，亲身体验教学，并观察孩子能否喜欢、顺应这种教学方式，能否主动参与学习。切身的感受和体验比什么都重要。

7, free English institutions are generally arranged free Demo class, ordinary parents can call the toll free telephone consultation, booking, choose to take the children to the field, the weekend, experience teaching, and to observe whether children love, conform to the teaching mode, can take the initiative to participate in the study. The feeling and experience of the body are more important than anything.

8、看价格、看性价比

8. Look at the price and see the price ratio

现在的很多家长形成了这样的惯性思维，以为价格越高的培训班，教学质量就会越好。其实事实不是这样的。价格高不一定他们的教学质量就见得好，之所以价格高，那是因为本身的教学成本高，比如师资投入大，因此，家长们不要养成价格高质量就好的坏习惯，而是要看性价比，同样质量比较价格，同样价格比服务，同样服务比附加值，这样比较下来，哪个机构更实惠更靠谱更合心意就很明显了。

Now many parents have formed such an inertial thinking, the higher the price of the training class, the better the quality of teaching. In fact, the fact is not the case. The high price is not necessarily their teaching quality is good, the price is high, it is because of the high cost of teaching itself, such as teaching investment, therefore, parents don't form the price high quality good and bad habits, but to look at the price, same quality price, the same price than service, the same service is added this value is down, which mechanism is more affordable and more reliable more desirable is obvious.

以上总结了8点，为家长们选择少儿英语培训机构仅作参考，在具体实际考察中能匹配上以上10条的，可以作为上上之选；如果要做出次优选择的话，也应该符合6条以上；如果只符合1-2条的话，那就不用考虑了。

The above sums up 8 points, selection of children's English training institutions for reference only for parents, can match the above 10 in the actual inspection, can be used as a good choice; if you want to make a suboptimal choice, should also be in accordance with Article 6 above; if only in accordance with Article 1-2, then you don't need considering the.
