初中生提高英语学习的方法

对初中生而言，英语是难度较大的学科，掌握英语学习的方法，使自己能独立获取知识，是取得优秀成绩的重要措施。下面北京外教网的外教老师再次为您分享提高初中生英语的方法：

For junior high school students, English is a difficult subject, mastering English learning methods, so that they can independently acquire knowledge, is an important measure to achieve good results. The following foreign teachers in Beijing foreign teachers to share with you again to improve the junior high school english:

第一，一定要有预习。预习可以使我们有心理准备，可以帮助我们提高课堂效率。对于自己老师的教学进度，我们应该比较清楚，所以我们应该提前预习第二天内容，可以充分了解自己的难点在何处，听课时就能够有的放矢。

First, we must have preview. Preview can make us have psychological preparation, can help us improve classroom efficiency. For his teacher's teaching progress, we should be more clear, so we should prepare in advance for second days, can fully understand their own difficulties in where and when they can be targeted lecture.

第二，提高课堂听课效率。在课堂上我们应该做到以下几点：1.静听。上课认真听讲，做好笔记，无论听、说、读、写都要使自己始终活跃。.2积极参与。英语是实践性强的学科，要大胆地练习，敢说、敢读，积极回答老师提出的问题。课堂上积极发言对我们是大有裨益的，每一次的积极发言都是一次听说训练，可以使我们集中精力，对较难或者模糊不清的问题有一个明确的答案。经常举手发言也可以让老师加深对自己的印象。课堂上的听讲至关重要，老师在讲到重点时总有自己的语言或者体态特点，我们要善于观察和把握。3勤动笔。把教师的重点及时迅速记在课堂记录本上，不过要处理好听课和记笔记的关系，不能顾此失彼。4多开口。对于自己课堂不清楚的地方，下课后一定要及时询问老师，把问题解决在当天，不要顾及所谓的面子而不懂装懂，否则漏洞越来越多，最后造成英语成绩的不断滑坡。

Second, improve the efficiency of classroom lectures. In class, we should do the following: 1. Listen carefully in class, take notes, listen, speak, read and write. .2 active participation. English is a practical discipline, to be bold practice, dare to speak, dare to read, and actively answer the questions raised by the teacher. It is good for us to speak actively in class, and every active speech is a kind of listening and speaking training, which can make us concentrate and have a clear answer to the difficult or vague questions. Often raise your hand can also let the teacher to deepen their impression. Listening to the classroom is crucial, the teacher talked about the focus of their own language or body characteristics, we must be good at observation and grasp. 3 more writing. The teacher's key promptly recorded in the classroom record book, but to deal with the relationship between the class listen and take notes, can not care for this and lose that. More than and 4 openings. For his class not clear place, after class must promptly asked the teacher, to solve the problem in the day, do not take into account the so-called face and smatter, otherwise the loophole is more and more, we finally caused landslides English achievement.

第三，课后必须及时复习

Third, must be timely review after class

这里的“及时”，包括下课后及时复习，三分钟的时间闭上眼睛回顾本课重点和难点，中午午休要再次复习，至多五分钟。晚上回家一定要及时再复习一遍。这个过程中如果用没有记住的地方一定要在笔记本上做好标记以备以后周复习和月复习时重点复习，这样也减轻了自己复习的负担，提高了复习的效率。这个过程中不是单纯的看书和看单词，而是自己考自己，本课的知识体系记住了没？本课的错题巩固住了吗？

Here the "timely", including the timely review after class, three minutes of time to close your eyes to review the key points and difficulties, noon lunch break to review again, up to five minutes. Home in the evening must review again. This process if no place to remember for later review and review key week month review mark in a notebook, it also reduces their review burden, improve the efficiency of the review. This process is not simple to read and look at the word, but their own test, the knowledge of the system to remember? The wrong topic consolidate to live?

第四，复习不是一天完成的，而是长线作战。

Fourth, the review is not a day to complete, but a long battle.

复习计划要长短结合，粗略相宜。每周六要把本周的内容复习一遍，每个月末把当月的知识巩固一遍。同时，随着考试体制的改革，局限课本是不行了。“见多”方能“识广”，“博大”才会“精深”，我们应多收听收看简单的电视英语节目，阅读适宜的课外读物，扩大阅读量，从而使课外知识成为课内知识的有益补充。

Review the plan should be combined with the length, rough fitting. Every Saturday to review the contents of this week, the end of each month to consolidate the knowledge of the month. At the same time, with the reform of the examination system, the limitations of textbooks is not enough. "See more" can "know", "great" will "intensive", we should be listening to watch English TV program simple, suitable for reading extracurricular books, expand the reading quantity, so that the extracurricular knowledge become a useful supplement of knowledge in class.

具体到每一天回到家后应该怎么做，一定要按照下面的方法进行，并一定要养成好的学习习惯。很多同学反映，明明已经把单词全部背过来了，结果老师提问时又忘记了。这说明我们的复习没有计划性和周期性。我们每天回到家后要做的事情可以归结为三个方面：听、读、写。听指的是把当天学习的单词、对话或者课文听两到三遍，争取耳熟能详，还要理解所听内容。中考英语的听力分数为二十分，可见听力的重要性。然后朗读当天学习的单词、句型、对话或者课文，必须要熟练。最后是要书写。要写的内容包括新单词，重点句子以及上课时老师举例说明单词或者词组用法的句子。下一步是完成英语家庭作业，这里需要说明的一点是一定要先复习完后再写作业，写作业不是为了应付老师，而是检查今天学习的内容掌握程度，这个过程中如果有问题，要把问题记在旁边，转天一定要及时问老师，要不会越积越多。最后是预习。

Specific to each day after returning home should do, must follow the following method, and must develop good study habits. A lot of students reflect, obviously have all the words back, the results of the teacher's question and forget. This shows that our review is not planned and periodic. Every day we come home to do things can be summed up in three areas: listening, reading, writing. Listen to refers to the study of the words, or dialog text listen to two to three times, but also for the understanding of what content for having heard it many times. The listening score of the test is twenty, which shows the importance of listening. Then read the day to learn the words, sentences, dialogue or text, must be skilled. The last is to write. To write the contents of the new words, key sentences and the class teacher to illustrate the use of words or phrases sentences. The next step is to finish the English homework, one thing to note here is sure to review after homework, homework is not to meet the teachers, but today learning content mastery, this process if there are problems, to put it down in the next day to, must be timely to ask the teacher do not accumulate. The last is preview.

外教网外教老师介绍学好外语还要记住以下几个原则

Foreign teachers to learn foreign language teachers should also remember the following principles

1．学习不中断，哪怕每天挤出10分钟也可以，早晨则是大好时机。

1 do not interrupt learning, even if you can squeeze out 10 minutes a day, the morning is a good opportunity.

2．学厌了可变换一下学习方式或形式。

2 learning to learn the way or form.

3．不要脱离下下文孤立地死记硬背。

3 don't leave the context words by rote.

4．随时记下并背熟那些常用的句型。

4 write down and memorize the commonly used sentences.

5．要敢于说外语，不要怕出错误，要请别人纠正，不要难为情，更不要泄气。

5 dare to speak a foreign language, don't be afraid to make mistakes, to ask others to correct, not embarrassed, not discouraged.

6．要坚信自己一定能够达到目的，坚信自己有坚强的毅力和学好外语的才能

6 to believe that they will be able to achieve their goals, firmly believe that they have a strong will and ability to learn a foreign language
