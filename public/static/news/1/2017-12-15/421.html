提高英语口语英语外教推荐这些方法

学英语最大的问题就是变成哑巴英语。很多人咨询英语外教老师：我学英语的时间并不比别人少啊，考试也都考的不错，怎么就是过不了英语口语这一关呢。其实这主要跟两个因素有关，一个是心理原因，害怕说错以及犯错带来的尴尬是主要原因，这个要靠自己克服;另一个原因就是英语表达技巧上的了，下面英语外教一对一老师就来简单的说一说。

The biggest problem in learning English is to become dumb English. Many people are consulting English teachers: I don't have less time to learn English than others, and they also do well in exams. In fact, this is mainly with the two factors, one is afraid of mistakes and wrong psychological reasons, the embarrassment is the main reason for this, rely on their own to overcome; another reason is that the English skills of English, the teacher one on one teacher to simply say.

1、不必太纠结语法，流利的表达更重要。

1. It's not necessary to get entangled in grammar. Fluency is more important.

Xiao Bian, like many people, is always worried when speaking English, is it wrong? Sentence patterns are not right? And so on. In fact, you don't have to tangle this, even if it is wrong, they can understand what you mean.

2.不要在心里对所要表达的话进行提前翻译。

2. do not translate in mind the words to be expressed in advance.

现在学英语很流行英英词典，外教认为我们每次在说话之前不要总想着中文，然后试图翻译这句中文，这就会使你的表达很慢。外教一对一建议学习者训练自己的英语思维，尽量用英语翻译英语。

Now English is very popular English English dictionary, foreign teachers think that every time we don't always think before speaking Chinese, then try to translate this sentence Chinese, this will make your expression is very slow. One to one foreign teacher recommends that the learners train their own English thinking and try to translate English in English as much as possible.

3.在英语环境中磨炼

3. in the English environment

说了这么多，如果你没有语言环境也是效果甚微。学习语言就是为了应用，有时间的话可以去参加一些英语角的活动，跟同伴进行角色扮演也是一个不错的方法。

So much, if you don't have a language environment, it's very small. Learning the language is for the purpose of application, and time can take part in some English corner activities. It is also a good way to play the role of the partner.

Watching movies is also recommended by many people. Before Xiaobian also wrote an article about how to see English movies to achieve the purpose of learning, now summarizes the following points.
