幼儿绘本有何作用?

家有小宝的都知道，要学习英语一定会先接触英语绘本，可是还有很多家长并不了解英语绘本到底是什么，读绘本到底有什么用。英语外教一对一今天就来跟大家详细的说说如何学习英语绘本。

Everyone in the family knows that learning English will contact English picture books first, but many parents don't know what the English picture book is and what's the use of picture book. English foreign teachers one on one today to talk to you in detail how to learn English picture books.

幼儿绘本有何作用?

What is the role of a child's picture book?

1、帮助宝宝认识事物。小孩子的好奇心强，对于未知的事物总是乐于观察和学习，而绘本就是他们认识世界的一个好方法。

1, help the baby to know things. Children are very curious and always willing to observe and learn about the unknown, and the picture book is a good way for them to know the world.

2、语言学习材料。读英语绘本的最终目的还是语言学习，通过绘本中的单词和听力，可以帮助孩子培养语感，积累语言素材。

2. Language learning materials. The final purpose of reading the English picture book is to learn the language. Through the words and hearing in the picture book, it can help the child cultivate the sense of language and accumulate language material.

3、有利于塑造孩子的思维习惯。绘本通常都带有一定的故事性，孩子的情绪也会跟着故事情节的发展而变，学习主人公性格中的优点，也是很有好处的。

3, it is conducive to the shaping of children's thinking habits. Picture books usually have a certain storytelling. Children's emotions will also change with the development of story plots. It's also good to learn the virtues of the hero's character.

4、审美教育。鲜艳的色彩，明亮的颜色，活灵活现的人物都是吸引小朋友读绘本的原因。

4, aesthetic education. Bright colors, bright colors, vivid characters are the reasons to attract children to read picture books.

婴幼儿早期进行英文绘本阅读的好处

The benefits of early childhood reading in English picture books

跟读绘本并模仿绘本里面的发音可以帮助孩子形成良好的发音习惯和纯正的口音。很多学英语多年的人都发现，发音太过于接近的词汇他们很难分辨清楚，但从小就学英语的人却可以轻松辨别。

Read the picture books and picture books which imitate pronunciation can help children to form a good pronunciation habit and pure accent. Many people who have learned English for many years have found that they are very difficult to distinguish between words that are too close to pronunciation, but people who have learned English from an early age can be easily identified.

另外，英语绘本可以说是最简单的原版英文书，里面的词汇和句型虽然简单，却都是非常地道的表达。原汁原味的语言有利于孩子形成语言学习的正确认知。

In addition, the English picture book can be said to be the simplest original English book. The words and sentence patterns are very simple, but they are all very authentic. The original language is beneficial to the children to form the correct cognition of language learning.

有利于英语学习兴趣的培养。学语言没有兴趣可不行，但是语言兴趣的培养却不是一件容易的事。家长们在挑选绘本的时候，可以根据孩子的性格特点挑选适合他们的绘本。外教一对一建议我们要注重语言学习的个性化教育，充分将学习与孩子的性格特点结合在一起。

It is beneficial to the cultivation of interest in English learning. It is not easy to learn language without interest, but the cultivation of language interest is not an easy thing. When choosing picture books, parents can choose a picture book suitable for them according to the character of their children. One to one foreign teacher suggests that we should pay attention to the individualized education of language learning and combine the study with the character of the children.
