成人该如何学好英文呢？来ForeignerHR

由于工作需要，个人兴趣等原因，成人学习英文的需求也越来越多。那么成人该如何学好英文呢？这里，外教小编也为大家总结出了很多建议，希望能对成人学习英文起到一定的帮助：

Due to the needs of work, personal interests and other reasons, the need for adults to learn English more and more. How to learn English? Here, the foreign teacher Xiao Bian also summed up a lot of suggestions for you, hoping to learn English for adults to play a certain help:

1、教材是必备品。

1, teaching materials are essential.

成人在大脑上与儿童有很大差异，而且也不可能有和现在孩子一样的学习外语的环境和精力，所以成人学习必须要有教材。选择一套好的教材，由浅入深、循序渐进地引导你进入英语的世界，你根本不用担心怎么去听说读写，跟着教材走就可以了。在加上外教一对一的指导，相信会让成人的学习达到事半功倍的作用。

There are a lot of differences between adults and children in the brain, and it is impossible to have the same environment and energy to learn a foreign language as a child. Choose a set of good teaching materials, and to guide you step by step from the shallower to the deeper into the world of English, you don't have to worry about how to read and write, you can go along with textbooks. In addition to foreign teachers one on one guidance, I believe will make adult learning to achieve the multiplier effect.

2、背诵文章也是一个妙招。

2, recite the article is also a coup.

学英语，背诵是必不可少的。背单词，背句型，背语法，背文章。最基础的就是背单词，同时背单词也是难度最大的。不少单词有好几种词性，每个词性又有好几种意思。背几个单词，远不如背由几十个单词组成的句子容易。而背几个独立的句子，不如背由几个句子组成的文章方便。所以从总体来看，反而是背文章效率最高了。文章背多了，一些表达就自认而然的会了。

Reciting is essential in learning english. Recite words, sentences, grammar, back articles. The most basic is to recite the word, and the word is also the most difficult. Many words have several parts of speech, each part of speech has several meanings. Back a few words, far less than the dozens of words from the back of the sentence easy. It's better to recite a few sentences with a few sentences. So from the overall point of view, but the article is the highest efficiency. The article back more, some expression on the admission and will be.

3、预习复习必不可少。

3, Preview Review essential.

每次课前预习本次上课要学的内容，把不懂的东西记下来，上课有针对的听，或者询问老师。课后再把老师本次上课的内容复习一遍，巩固所学的知识。这样反复巩固，加深印象，养成良好的预习、复习习惯。您的学习效率一定会棒棒哒。

Before each lesson preview this lesson to learn the content, do not know what to write down, there is a class for the purpose of listening, or ask the teacher. After class, the teacher to review the contents of this lesson again, to consolidate the knowledge learned. This repeatedly consolidate, deepen the impression, develop good preview, review habits. Your learning efficiency will bang bang da.

4、创造英文环境。

4, create English environment.

一个好的英文环境也能为您的英文学习起到助力作用。平时工作闲暇时间可以多听英文歌，看英文剧集，收听英文广播，看英文书籍报纸。看到一个东西，首先想想它的英文表达。家里的一些摆设什么的，也可以尽情增加一些英文的。让英文在您的生活里无处不在，创造好的英文环境。

A good English environment can help you learn english. I can listen to English songs, watch English drama, listen to English radio, read English books and newspapers. When you see something, think of it first. Some of the furnishings at home, or you can enjoy some english. Make English everywhere in your life and create a good English environment.
