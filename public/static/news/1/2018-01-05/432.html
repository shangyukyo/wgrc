口语学习最重要的是要有好的语言环境和多练习

想说一口标准的英语是每个英语学习者的梦想，在英语学习过程中，口语学习也是最让学习者困扰的部分。北京外教英语口语外教培训专家指出，口语学习最重要的是要有好的语言环境和多练习。

It is the dream of every English learner to say a standard English. In the process of learning English, oral English is the most perplexing part of the learners. Beijing foreign teacher training experts point out that the most important thing in oral learning is to have a good language environment and more practice.

很多自学英语的人很难说出一口标准英语是因为：大多数自学者都是盲目地读课文，认为这就是练习口语；盲目地跟读光盘、磁带，认为只要跟读就能够纠正发音；每天坚持背单词、记语法、题海战，就能把英语口语学好。然而当真正应用的时候却开不了口，外教英语口语培训老师强调，其实学英语就应该“浸泡”在纯英文的语言环境中，多说多听多交流！

A lot of people learning English is very difficult to say a standard English is that: since the majority of scholars are reading the text blindly, think this is to practice oral English; blindly read CDs and tapes, as long as you can read the correct pronunciation; to back words, grammar, lots of exercises every day, will be able to learn oral English well. However, when it is applied, it can not be opened. Foreign language oral English training teacher emphasizes that actually learning English should be "soaked" in pure English language environment, speak more, listen more and communicate more.

北京外教采用外教一对一教学模式，让学员和外教每堂课都有深入的英语口语交流，把英语作为语言真正活用起来，多说多听多交流，循序渐进提升英语口语能力！另外，外教均来自欧美英语母语国家，其地道标准的英语发音、正确的英语表达，可以让学员完全“浸泡”在纯英文的语言环境中，耳濡目染，从而达到用英语交际和思维的习惯，让流利英语口语脱口而出！

Beijing foreign teachers use one to one teaching mode of foreign teachers, so that students and foreign teachers can have in-depth oral English communication in every class, and use English as a real language, speak more, listen more and communicate more, improve oral English step by step. In addition, foreign teachers are from English speaking countries in Europe and the United States, the authentic standard English pronunciation, correct English expression, so that students can completely immersed in pure English language environment, monasteries, so as to achieve in English communication and thinking habits, make fluent oral English blurt out!

语言最终用于交流，反复模仿和实践才能熟练应用。大量的模仿和实践尤为重要，首先，自己的外教就是最佳模仿对象；另外，可以多看些原声电影，平时也尽量和外教多沟通。汉英语课程的设置全部以增强学员实践运用、多交流为主旨，包括外教沙龙课、外教口语环境课、影视欣赏课等，通过多种教学方式科学结合，循序渐进的学习让英语口语自然而然脱口而出。

Language is finally used for communication, repeated imitation and practice can be used skillfully. A lot of imitation and practice are particularly important. First of all, their own foreign teachers are the best imitation objects. In addition, we can see more original movies and usually try to communicate with foreign teachers as much as possible. Chinese and English courses are all set up to enhance student practice, more exchanges for the purpose, including salon class, oral English class environment, film appreciation courses, through a variety of teaching methods combined with scientific, progressive learning spoken English naturally blurt out.
