一对一外教,选对方法最重要_外教_ForeignerHR

在孩子学英语这条路上，相信每位中国妈妈都有一部血泪史。如何让孩子在潜移默化中自动自觉地坚持把英文学好？外教分享：其实，只要找对方法，学习就是一件快乐的事。

The children learn English in this way, I believe that every mother China has a tragic history. How to let the children consciously and unconsciously insist on learning English well? Foreign teachers share: in fact, as long as finding the right method, learning is a happy thing.

我们来看看一个人的母语学习过程

Let's look at a person's native language learning process

10个月时，开始意识听到声音

At 10 months, I began to hear the sound

1岁开始意识到语言与事物的联系

At the age of 1, I began to realize the connection between language and things

1岁半只会说单个的名词

1 and a half would say a single NOUN

3岁左右完全学会了说话

About 3 years old, completely learned to speak

6岁开始简单的阅读、识字

At the age of 6, simple reading, literacy

10岁开始简单的写作

Start simple writing at the age of 10

首先大量听，然后自然说，接着识字、认字，最后写作，这就是母语学习的过程！母语学习成果：短短两年多就能熟练交流，自然轻松、不知不觉中就学会，听、说、读、写全面攻破，一旦掌握，终身不忘。

First listen, then naturally said, then read, read, and writing, this is the language learning process! Mother tongue learning results: just two years, you can skilled communication, natural easy, unknowingly learned, listening, speaking, reading and writing, comprehensive break, once mastered, lifelong forget.

现在，父母越来越希望孩子能讲一口标准的英语，但是做了很多口语练习，最终发现进步缓慢。三岁小孩讲母语都能非常流畅，为什么英语这么难讲？背后的原理其实是：只要听得懂英语，说会很容易很自然；如果听力不行，练再多口语都很难练好！

Nowadays, more and more parents want their children to speak standard English, but they do a lot of oral practice and eventually find that they are making slow progress. Three year old child can speak native language very fluent, why English is so difficult? The principle behind this is: as long as you understand English, it will be easy and natural. If you don't hear well, it will be difficult to practice!

4到12岁是练习英语听力的最佳时间，方法正确一听就懂

4 to 12 years old is the best time to practice English listening. The method is correct and you understand it

来到一个新国家，一年左右小朋友就能听懂当地的外语了，出门就能做父母的翻译了。但是很多成年人在国外几年都未必能听得懂当地外语。因为4―12岁的小朋友大脑处于发育过程中，还没有定型，所以学外语听力非常快，很容易就能听懂外语了，但是过了这个年龄，大脑发育定型，学英语想听懂，就会变得越来越难了。

Come to a new country, a year or so, children can understand the local foreign language, and go out to become parents translation. But many adults may not be able to understand the local foreign language for several years abroad. Because the 4 - 12 year old child brain in the development process, has not been finalized, so learn foreign language listening very fast, very easy to understand a foreign language, but after this age, shaping brain development, learning English to understand, will become more and more difficult.

所以，学英语，找对方法很重要，在最佳的年纪学习英语也是很重要的，不要认为孩子小，接受不了，小孩子记性才更好的！

So, it's important to find the right way to learn English. It's also important to learn English at the best age. Don't think it's better for a child to accept it than a child!
