欧美外教一对一谈英语听力提高有方法

对于英语这门学科来讲，听力其实是其中最容易拿分的部分，但是为什么经常有同学会觉得听力反而成了自己老是失分的一块心病呢?希望通过分享我的一点经验能对大家有帮助。下面欧美外教一对一老师为大家简单介绍。

For the subject of English speaking, listening is one of the most difficult points, but why students often feel listening has become a heart disease I always lost? Hope can help you to share my experience. One to one teacher is a brief introduction to the European and American foreign teachers.

第一，生活中培养习惯。

First, cultivate habits in life.

听力是必须经过练习的。想一下为什么我们听母语能那么轻松。原因只有一个，我们经常处在这样的一种环境下，家人、同学以及老师都是用母语交流，所以一定要多听。对于提高自己的听力来说不能只靠做平常考试的练习，为了培养总体听力的感觉，我会准备一些泛听材料比如BBC，VOA，每天起床后和睡觉前我都用录音机放着，不管是不是在认真听，是不是听得懂，都让它放着，给自己一个多听的大环境。

Listening must be practiced. Think about why we can listen to the mother tongue so easily. There is only one reason. We are often in such an environment where family, classmates and teachers are all communicating in their mother tongue, so we must listen to them more. To improve your listening, do not rely on the usual practice exam, in order to cultivate the overall sense of hearing, I will prepare some listening materials such as BBC, VOA, wake up every day and before I go to bed with the tape recorder, regardless of whether or not to be listening, is not understand, let it out listen, give yourself a big environment.

第二，考试时做听力题的方法。

Second, the way to do the listening problem in the exam.

考试时千万别紧张，因为考试时需要专注，如果你紧张了可能会漏掉其中的一点条件。另外，考试时最好将整道题的问题甚至是所有给你的条件用缩写符号记录下来，老师教的这个方法虽然开始时感觉有些麻烦，还会影响速度，但是现在我已经觉得它成为了一种方法，不仅能够加深你对这道题的理解更能让你有事可做，不会在听完后漫无目的地等着下一题，那样反而可能走神。所以考试时一定要让自己始终保持在一种放松但同时专注的状态下。

Don't be nervous when you take a test, because you need to focus on the exam. If you are nervous, you may miss some of the conditions. In addition, the exam will be the best whole questions and even give you all the conditions of abbreviations and symbols to record this teacher although started feeling some trouble, but also affect the speed, but now I think it has become a kind of method, not only can deepen your understanding of this problem more can give you something to do, not in the end aimlessly waiting for the next question, but that may be distracted. So you must always keep yourself in a state of relaxation but at the same time.

第三，研究透听力的题型。

Third, study the type of listening comprehension.

这个方法在其他很多学科都有应用，但估计很少有人真正地系统地把它应用到英语听力中，可能有时我们做题时会发现，这道题没放之前我看答案就大概知道它要问什么了，我经常用它配合第二点讲的缩写符号这个习惯，就可以很轻松地分析出答案大概是什么，这样就可以在一定程度上提升答案的准确度。多多培养这种能力，能从一定程度上提高听力效率和成绩。

This method is applied in many other disciplines, but very few real system to apply it to English listening, sometimes we do will find that this problem is not the answer I put before you probably know it to ask what, I often use it with the second point of abbreviations and symbols this habit, you can easily analyze what is probably the answer, so it can improve the answer in a certain degree of accuracy. The ability to cultivate this ability can improve listening efficiency and performance to a certain extent.

第四，需要明确一点，必须坚持，有恒心。

Fourth, it needs to be clear, must adhere to, with perseverance.

这是大前提，短时间内听力练起来的确不轻松，但是如同母语一样，一旦你练成了，这种能力不仅是为了成绩，更是可以陪伴你一生的，所以我觉得花时间去提高听力是非常值得的。最后我祝愿大家能尽快提高听力!

This is a major premise. Listening practice is not easy in a short time. But like your mother tongue, once you have been trained, this ability is not only for achievement, but also for your life. So I think it's worth saving time to improve listening. Finally, I wish you all of you to improve your hearing as soon as possible!
