幼儿英语启蒙,你了解多少？_外教_ForeignerHR

幼儿学英语的初期是需要进行英语启蒙的，这也算是英语学习的一个基石，但是幼儿英语启蒙到底有多重要，又该如何对孩子进行英语启蒙，家长们又知道多少呢?

Early childhood learning English is the need for enlightenment in English, this is a cornerstone of learning English, but English Enlightenment how important, how the English Enlightenment for children, parents also know how much?

首先跟随外教小编一起来看看儿童英语启蒙的重要性

First of all, follow the foreign teacher's small book together to see the importance of children's English Enlightenment

有很多家长表示自己已经上初中或者高中的孩子英语不好，成绩一直是处于中下的水平，一部分的原因是由于他们的学习方法不对或者不够勤奋，但大部分的原因是由于他们小时候的英语没有学好。想想看，有哪个中国的小孩到初中还不会说流利的汉语呢?也许你会说这跟母语环境有很大的关系，没错，但跟语言启蒙的关系同样很大。

There are a lot of parents said their children have a middle school or junior high school English achievement is not good, has been in the middle and lower levels, partly because of their learning methods or not hard enough, but most of the reason is because they didn't learn good English when I was young. Think about it. Which Chinese child does not speak fluent Chinese in junior high school? Maybe you'll say it has a lot to do with the mother tongue environment. Yes, but it's as much about language enlightenment as it is.

每一个健康孩子生下来有1亿个脑细胞，每个脑细胞组成了2万个连接点，这些脑细胞能否形成连接主要取决于孩子是否生活在一个丰富的环境中。如果让孩子在很小的时候就开始接触英语和汉语，让他们同时学习两种语言，这不仅不会造成他们的学习负担，相反的，会更加刺激孩子的大脑发育，激发他们的语言潜能。

Every healthy child is born with 100 million brain cells, each of which consists of 20 thousand connections, and whether these brain cells can form connections depends largely on whether the child lives in a rich environment. If your child is very small when you started to learn English and Chinese, let them learn two languages at the same time, it would not cause their learning burden, on the contrary, will be more to stimulate the child's brain development, stimulate their potential of language.

研究表明，学习第二语言的孩子，长大后会更加聪明，因为他们从小就锻炼了在不同的语言环境中切换思维，处理问题的能力也更强。让孩子早期接触英语并接受正规的英语学习，不但可以开发孩子的大脑学习潜力，而且会让孩子在今后的社会激烈竞争中占据优势。英语作为一门特殊的语种，在孩子们中考、高考乃至更高层次的学习(如考研、出国)中起着举足轻重的作用。

Studies have shown that children who learn a second language are more intelligent when they grow up, because they exercise as a child, switch their minds in different language environments, and are more capable of dealing with problems. Let children early exposure to English and formal English learning, not only can develop children's brain learning potential, and will let the children in the future fierce competition in society to take advantage. As a special language, English plays an important role in children's entrance examination, college entrance examination and even higher level of study (for example, postgraduate entrance examination, going abroad).

对于零基础的孩子来说，英语启蒙首先要解决的是什么问题呢?

For zero based children, what is the first thing that English Enlightenment should solve?

听力!

Hearing!

英语启蒙其实就是通过听力来启蒙的。就像中国的小孩学汉语，肯定不是等到小学学汉字、学拼音才是学汉语，而是他们在妈妈肚子里的时候、在出生以后所接触的所有汉语声音的时候就已经开始学习了。

English Enlightenment is actually enlightenment through listening. As Chinese children learn Chinese, certainly not until primary school, Chinese characters learn pinyin is to learn Chinese, but they all Chinese voice, when in the womb after birth in contact had been studied.

听说，听说，就是听在先，说在后。想要给孩子做英语启蒙，就要在孩子小的时候就创造一些语言环境，比如带他们听英语儿歌，播放简单的英语动画片，说英语单词等。不要觉得孩子还小，好像什么都不懂，其实你所做的都会影响他们。

I heard that listening and speaking is listening first, saying later. Want to give English Enlightenment to children, it is necessary to create some language environment when children are young, such as take them to listen to English songs, play simple English cartoons, speak English words and so on. Don't feel like kids are young. You don't seem to know anything. What you do will affect them.

说和应用

Speaking and Application

有效的输出，说和应用也必不可少。会唱英语儿歌、会读英语绘本的孩子有很多，但是跟开口说英语还是有区别的。家长除了要给孩子练习听力之外，还要提供尽可能多的机会让孩子说英语。学英语的环境非常重要，如果家长不能在家里给孩子提供一个学习环境的话，可以找少儿英语外教老师陪孩子学习，也就是让孩子接触更专业、更系统的的英语启蒙和学习。

Effective output, as well as application, is also essential. There are many children who can sing English songs and read English picture books, but there is still a difference between speaking English and speaking english. In addition to giving children practice listening, parents also offer as many opportunities as possible for their children to speak english. The English environment is very important, if parents are not at home for the children to provide a learning environment, you can find the children's English teacher to accompany the child to learn, also is to let the children contact more professional, more systematic English learning and enlightenment.
