有趣又有效教孩子记单词

在国外一份两百多万人参与的测试结果显示中，以英语为母语的成年人的平均词汇量在两万至三万五千，8岁儿童的平均词汇量在一万左右，4岁儿童的平均词汇量在五千左右。

In a foreign country, about two million people participated in the test. The results showed that the average vocabulary size of English speaking adults was between twenty thousand and thirty-five thousand, the average vocabulary size of 8 year olds was around ten thousand, and the average vocabulary size of 4 year olds was around five thousand.

在我们国内最常见的英语考试中，四级只要求四千，六级为六千左右，考研英语的大纲要求也不超过六千。如此一比较，国内要求掌握的词汇量可以说很少了。可是为什么大家还是无法有效掌握这些单词呢？问题出在哪里？以英语为母语的人是怎么记忆英语单词的？

In the most common English test in our country, the four level is only four thousand, and the six is about six thousand. The requirements for the English examination are not more than six thousand. In this comparison, the amount of vocabulary mastered at home is very few. But why can't you master these words effectively? Where is the problem? How do English - speaking people remember English words?

外教老师说，他们的词汇量大部分来源于小学至高中时期的大量阅读。之前我们来自美国的幼儿外教老师就说过，无论是小学生还是中学生，他们的阅读量都是很大的。老师会给他们列长长的阅读清单，并写出阅读报告。

Foreign teachers say their vocabulary is largely derived from a large number of reading from primary school to high school. Our preschool teachers from the United States have said that both primary and secondary school students have a large amount of reading. The teacher will give them a long list of reading and write a reading report.

由此可见，在阅读中记忆单词才是最有效的记单词方法。我们的幼儿外教老师就是在英语绘本的阅读中让孩子记单词的。

It can be seen that remembering words in reading is the most effective way to remember words. Our preschool teacher is to let the child remember the words in the reading of the English picture book.

在阅读中，孩子会潜移默化地学习单词，句型。所以想让孩子掌握有效的词汇，就让孩子多读英语绘本吧。在大量阅读的基础上再辅之以适当的记忆方法，轻松拥有可观的有效词汇量。

In reading, the child will learn the words and sentence patterns imperceptibly. So in order to let the child master the effective vocabulary, let the child read more English picture book. On the basis of a large number of reading, supplemented by appropriate memory methods, it is easy to have a considerable amount of effective vocabulary.

单词分类记忆法

Word classification memory method

单词分类记忆法，就是一种不错的记忆方式。比如把孩子阅读过的绘本里的单词进行比较分类，归纳整理。使零乱的知识系统化。如数字、动物、植物、衣服、水果、交通工具、职业、衣服、日期等等。

Word classification memory method is a good way of remembering. For example, the words in the picture book that children have read are classified and classified. The knowledge system disorder. Such as numbers, animals, plants, clothes, fruits, transportation, occupations, clothes, dates and so on.

做游戏也是记单词的好方法

Playing games is also a good way to remember words

游戏可以给孩子们快乐、经验、学识、思想、健康。游戏不仅能活跃气氛，调节孩子的学习情绪，更重要的是能培养孩子的英语学习兴趣，从而使孩子每天坚持听说读写的训练。久而久之，形成一种良好的英语学习习惯。在教学中，就阅读过的绘本内容，和孩子一起做相应的游戏是外教老师经常采用的教学方式。孩子们很喜欢游戏，而且学习的效果也很好。

Games can give children happiness, experience, knowledge, thought, and health. Games can not only activate the atmosphere, regulate children's learning emotions, but also cultivate children's interest in learning English, so that children can persist in training in listening, speaking, reading and writing every day. For a long time, a good habit of learning English is formed. In teaching, to read the picture book content, and children to do the corresponding game is often used by teachers of foreign teachers teaching methods. Children like games very much, and the effect of learning is very good.

外教提示，快放弃错误的背单词方式，拿起喜欢的英语原文书，阅读吧。阅读的图书先从简单的入手，然后循序渐进。阅读才是最好的扩充词汇量的方式哦。

Teach the foreign teachers, give up the wrong way of words, pick up your favorite English text, and read it. The books that are read start with a simple start, and then step by step. Reading is the best way to expand the vocabulary.
