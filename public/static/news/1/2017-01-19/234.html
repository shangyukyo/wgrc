对英语初学者如何选择适合自己当前水平的英语外教

对英语初学者来说，外教最好懂一些中文。对于已经有一定程度的朋友，选择不会中文的外教对提高英文听说水平更为有利。

For English beginners, foreign teachers had better understand some chinese. For those who have a certain degree of friends, choose not to speak Chinese foreign teachers to improve the level of English listening and speaking more favorable.

为什么外教要会中文呢？有些人说我请外教上课就是为了纯英语的环境，我要锻炼我的英文习惯，我希望课堂里外教一点中文都不说。对这些人的想法我只认可一部分，因为那要根据你的英语水平来定。如果你的水平还不错，能够听懂外籍老师用通俗易懂的英语来解释复杂的单词意思或者是语法，那可以选个不会汉语的，这样确实是打造英语氛围的好方法。

Why do foreign teachers want to speak Chinese? Some people say I asked foreign teachers to pure English environment, I will exercise my English habit, I hope to teach a class inside and outside Chinese did not say. I'm only a part of the idea of these people, because it depends on your english. If your level is also good, can understand the foreign teacher to explain complex words or grammar in plain English, it can choose not Chinese, this is indeed a good method to create the English atmosphere.
