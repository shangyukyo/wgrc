外教一对一教学更容易培养学习兴趣

现在的英语越来越重要，学习英语的人也越来越多，学习英语的方式也更加丰富起来，既有线下英语培训的，也有线上的英语培训机构，有小班模式的，也有“一对一”的英语教学模式。而且现在这种“外教一对一”的教学模式更受大众的喜爱。但又有一部分人在质疑，有没有“一对一”的必要性，‘一对一’教学真的有效吗？下面我们来分析一下“一对一”是很有必要的，而且效果很好！

Now English is more and more important, more and more people learn English, the way to learn English is more enriched, the English training under the existing lines, but also the English training institutions on the line, there are small class model, there are also "one to one" English teaching mode. And now this "foreign teacher one to one" teaching mode is more popular with the public. But some people are questioning whether there is a need for "one to one", is "one to one" teaching really effective? Here we analyze the "one to one" is very necessary, and the effect is very good!

一：外教一对一教学更容易培养学习兴趣

A: one to one teaching foreign teachers easier to cultivate interest in learning

无论是成人还是孩子，一开始对英语的兴趣并不是很浓烈，特别是在学校里学习的时候，学校里一个班级那么多的学生，而老师只有一双眼睛，根本照顾不来所有的学生。而ForeignerHR外教的纯外教一对一教学就不一样，外教可以根据你的性格为你制定适合你的学习方案，真正的做到因材施教，在学习的过程中，外教可以通过了解到学员的爱好，把上课的内容和一些最新的信息集合起来讲给学员听，学员就会对英语产生很大的兴趣，从而自然学习得更好。

Whether adults or children, the beginning of interest in English is not very strong, especially in the school study, a class in the school so many students, and the teacher has only one eye, can not take care of all students. ForeignerHR pure foreign teacher one on one teaching is not the same, teachers can according to your character as you make your study plan, really do teaching, in the process of learning, teachers can understand the students to love, to set the class content and some of the latest information up to listen to the students, the students will have a great interest in English, so naturally learn better.

二：外教一对一教学更好的提高学习效率

Two: foreign teacher one to one teaching, improve learning efficiency

学习英语的人有一个共同的特点，就是自控能力不是很好，一般在被忽视的情况下就容易发呆走神，‘一对多’的教育方式就有这个缺点，而外教一对一的模式，外教和学员进行时时交流，也会在第一时间里发现学员的问题，并将其指证出来，让学员得到更好的教育，学习效率自然会提高很多。

English people have a common characteristic, is self-control is not very good, generally neglected cases easily distracted in a daze, "one to many" education methods have the disadvantages, but teach one to one model, teachers and students always exchange, also will find the issues of students for the first time, and to testify, let students get a better education, learning efficiency will be greatly improved.

三：外教一对一教学可以得到更多开口和思考的机会

Three: foreign teacher one to one teaching can get more openings and thinking opportunities

英语学习是一个交流和开口说的过程，只有经常说，英语语感才会形成，学习英语才会更加快，更加的高效。而大班化的教育却提供不了这样的机会，相反，ForeignerHR外教英语外教一对一的教育模式，外教就和学员一个人交流，也可以形成一个很好的语言交流环境，这样，开口的机会会多很多，学起来自然也会快了！

English learning is a process of communication and speaking. Only when we often say that English language sense will form, English learning will be faster and more efficient. The class of education is not to provide such opportunities, on the contrary, ForeignerHR English teacher one on one mode of education, teachers and students have a personal communication, can also form a good language environment, so that the opening would be a lot more, learn to naturally fast!

总的来说，‘外教一对一’教学还是好处多多的，交流多一点，问题就少一点，英语进步也会更快一点

Generally speaking, the teaching of "one to one" is much more beneficial, more exchanges, less problems, and faster progress in English
