如何给孩子做英语启蒙呢?

如何给孩子做英语启蒙呢?外教老师建议年龄小的孩子做英语启蒙的时候着重听力这一块，主要可以让孩子听以下几个方面的听力资料。

How to do English Enlightenment for children? Foreign teachers recommend that young children do English Enlightenment when listening to this piece, the main can let children listen to the following aspects of listening materials.

一、儿歌最受小朋友喜欢

First, children's favorite children's songs

在听儿歌之前家长们首先要明确听的目的，并不是说听几首儿歌孩子就能流利的说英语了，这属于“急功近利”。外教建议我们的孩子听儿歌主要是培养孩子对于英语的韵律感和节奏感，是孩子学英语的基础。

Before listening to children's songs, parents should first clear the purpose of listening, not to say that listening to a few songs, children can speak English fluently, which belongs to "eager for quick success and instant benefit"". Foreign teachers suggest that our children listen to children's songs, mainly to cultivate children's rhythm and rhythm of English, is the basis for children to learn english.

听儿歌一般可以分两个步骤进行，一是泛听，选择各种各样的题材让孩子试听，找到孩子喜欢的形式。二是学唱，让孩子学唱一些他们非常感兴趣的儿歌，陪着他们一起唱，引导他们。边唱边跳可以说是比较好的学习形式，不至于让启蒙学习过于枯燥。

Listen to songs can generally be divided into two steps, one is listening, choose a variety of themes to let children audition, find the child in the form of love. The two is to learn to sing, let children learn to sing some of them are very interested in children's songs, accompany them to sing, guide them. Singing and dancing can be said to be a better form of learning, not to make enlightenment learning too boring.

二、童谣

Two, nursery rhymes

童谣与儿歌有异曲同工之妙，小朋友学习起来比较容易，也更能引起他们的兴趣。当孩子找到自己喜欢的童谣后，我们的外教老师一般都会创建一个学习场景，让孩子感觉身处童谣的环境中，也就是情景式口语教学，这种教学方法可以充分激发孩子的学习兴趣。

Children's rhymes are similar to children's songs, and it is easier for children to learn and also arouse their interest. When the children find their own love rhymes, our foreign teacher will create a learning environment in the scene, let the children feel that nursery rhymes, situational English teaching, this teaching method can fully arouse children's interest in learning.

童谣一般比较适合年龄偏小的孩子，如果年龄大的孩子不太喜欢一定不要勉强。要知道，所有的学习都应该建立在孩子的兴趣之上，勉强只会让孩子对英语学习产生排斥，适得其反。

Nursery rhymes are generally more suitable for younger children, if the older children do not like it, must not reluctantly. You know, all learning should be based on the child's interest, and will only allow children to exclude English learning, counterproductive.

三. 动画音频

Three. Animated audio

小朋友都喜欢看动画片，如果只是简单的看动画也就是一个娱乐的功能，但如果可以利用动画学英语何乐而不为呢?现在网络上也有很多适合孩子英语启蒙的动画片，还会根据孩子的年龄段进行细分。建议家长们可以找一些适合孩子英语启蒙的动画片，每天抽出一点时间陪孩子学习。很多经典的动画片都是取景于日常生活，而且场景多变，还具有一定的故事性。看动画的时间不宜过长，一般每天30分钟左右比较好，如果小朋友已经有一定的英语基础，可以在孩子看完动画后，鼓励小朋友将今天看的情节简单的复述给爸爸听，得到夸赞后的小朋友会更喜欢这样的形式。

Kids love to watch cartoons, if it is just a simple animation is also a function of entertainment, but if you can use the animation to learn English Why not?? now the network also has a lot of enlightenment for children English cartoons, will be subdivided according to the age of the child. It is suggested that parents can find some cartoons suitable for children's English Enlightenment, and take a little time every day to study with their children. A lot of classic cartoons are framing in daily life, and the scene is changeable, but also has a certain story. Look at the animation time should not be too long, generally every 30 minutes is better, if the children have some foundation in English, can read in children after the animation, to encourage the children today will see simple plot repeat to Dad, get praise after the kids prefer this form.

四. 绘本音频(经典绘本+分级读物)

Four. Picture books audio (classic picture book + graded reading)

英语绘本阅读是现在非常受欢迎的英语启蒙方式，当然了，英语绘本不仅可以读，还可以配合音频一起听。孩子自己学习不免枯燥，而且年龄太小的孩子也不太有自制力，但是如果可以跟爸爸妈妈一起阅读和听英语绘本，他们就会很高兴。毕竟每个孩子都希望得到父母的称赞，希望和自己最亲近的人在一起。

English picture book reading is now a very popular way of English Enlightenment, of course, English picture books can not only read, but also listen to the audio. Children learn by themselves are boring, and young children are not very self-control, but if you can read and listen to English picture books with your parents, they will be very happy. After all, every child wants to be praised by his parents, and he wants to be with his closest person.

当孩子读过一定数量的绘本，对英语已经有基础之后，就可以直接听分级读物的音频了。因为分级读物是以认读为目的的，所以语言简单，很多时候整本书就是一个句型，反复练习。练习裸听也是需要一个过程的，当孩子可以完全听懂后就会有成就感，学习也更有动力。

When the child has read a certain number of picture books, after the English already has the foundation, you can directly listen to the classification of the audio content of the book. Because graded reading is for reading purposes, so the language is simple, many times the whole book is a sentence pattern, repeated practice. Practice listening to naked also need a process, when the child can fully understand, there will be a sense of accomplishment, learning is more dynamic.
