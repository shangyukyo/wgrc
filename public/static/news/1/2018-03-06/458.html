成人英语学习方法大PK

成人英语学习与少儿英语完全不同，有很多成年人都是有英语基础的，也有部分是零基础，他们学习的目的一般是为了旅游、工作或者日常。商务英语外教表示，成年人无论是记忆力还是模仿力都不如小朋友，但是在学习方法上绝对是成人更胜一筹。

Adult English learning is quite different from children's English. Many adults have English basis, and some of them are zero based. Their purpose of learning is generally for tourism, work or daily life. Business English foreign teachers say that adults are not as good as their children in memory or imitation, but they are definitely better than adults in their learning methods.

成人英语学习主要有哪些方法呢?英语外教老师罗列了以下几种方法：

What are the main methods for adult English learning? English foreign teachers have listed the following methods:

方法一：网络学习法

Method 1: network learning method

适宜人群：自制能力强

Suitable population: strong self-control

网络学习的优点显而易见：不受时间和空间的限制，只要你有时间有网络就可以学习。网络学习可以实现资源的最大化利用，你可以自由选择自己喜欢的老师，喜欢的课程，非常自主和灵活。对于性格内向的人来说，网络学习也有一个好处，那就是无需拘谨，可以在网络上自由大胆的向老师和同学提问。另外，网络学习可以直播也可以是录播课，遇到不懂的或者难点，你可以选择多听几遍。

The advantages of network learning are obvious: it is not limited by time and space, so long as you have time to have a network, you can learn. Online learning can maximize the use of resources, you can freely choose your favorite teachers, favorite courses, very independent and flexible. For introverted people, there is also a benefit of online learning. That is to be free and bold enough to ask questions to teachers and classmates on the Internet. In addition, the network learning can broadcast can also be taped courses, or you do not understand the problem, you can choose to listen to a few times.

方法二：英语培训班

Method two: English training class

适宜人群：自制力较弱，学习主动性差，需要人督促的。

Suitable crowd: the self-control is weak, the learning initiative is poor, needs the person to urge.

有大班课也有小班课，有中教老师授课也有外教老师的。一般是将不同年龄，差不多层次的学生组合成一个班，在固定的时间和地点上课。课程有固定的教学体系，老师的教学也是以大部分人的需求为主，不太能顾及到个人需求。

There are also small classes in large class classes. There are teachers from foreign teachers as well as teaching teachers. In general, students of different ages and levels are combined into a class to attend classes at a fixed time and place. The curriculum has a fixed teaching system, and the teacher's teaching is mainly based on the needs of most people, and it can not take into account personal needs too.

ForeignerHR外教有专门针对成年人的外教一对一课程，我们会针对个人学习基础和特征设置个性化的课程，保证课程的针对性和效果。

ForeignerHR foreign teachers have a one to one course for foreign teachers, and we will set personalized courses for individual learning foundation and characteristics, so as to ensure the pertinence and effect of courses.

方法三：在家自学

Method three: self study at home

适宜人群：超强的自制力和学习能力

Suitable crowd: Super self-control and learning ability

自由性最强的一种学习方法。自由性强：学习时间和学习内容完全是自己决定，不收任何人的约束。在家自学对于成年人的要求很高，最好是有一定的学习基础，学习目的明确，能够督促自己学习的人。要想在这样的学习环境中取得良好的学习效果，成人英语外教老师建议在学习前制定学习计划，自己对自己有一个约束。

One of the most liberal methods of learning. Freedom is strong: the learning time and the content of learning are entirely their own decisions, and do not accept the constraints of anyone. Self-study at home is very demanding for adults. It is better to have a certain learning basis, a clear purpose of learning, and a person who can supervise his study. If we want to achieve good learning results in such a learning environment, adult English teachers recommend that they make a learning plan before learning, and have a constraint on themselves.
